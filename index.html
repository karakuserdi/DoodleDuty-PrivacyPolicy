Privacy Policy

HealthPaws values the privacy of its users. This Privacy Policy provides information on how HealthPaws collects, uses, and shares your personal data.

Information We Collect

Our app collects only your location information and pet health data in order for you to be able to use the app. This information is used to make the application within the app more effective for you. We do not collect any personal identifying information about you.

Use of Information

Your location information is used solely to personalize your experience, such as locating local vets and tracking your pet's exercise routines. Your pet's health data is used to facilitate tracking of medical treatments and personalized reminders. This information is not shared or used anywhere else.

Subscription Data

When you choose to subscribe to our service, you agree to our terms and conditions and authorize us to charge your payment method on a recurring basis. We do not store or have access to your payment details, as payments are processed through the Apple App Store.

Sharing of Information

Our app does not share the information we collect with third parties. We do not sell or distribute your personal data or your pet's health data.

Storage of Information

All the information we collect is stored locally on your device only. If you uninstall the app, all collected data will be permanently deleted. Our app does not store or use the collected data anywhere else.

Location Permission

Our app requires location permission from your device to function properly. This information is used solely to improve the functionality of the app and is not shared.

Validity of this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy is only applicable to the HealthPaws application. Other applications or websites may have different Privacy Policies.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. When changes are made, the new Privacy Policy will be posted on this page.
