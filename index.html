Privacy Policy for HealthPaws App

At HealthPaws App, one of our top priorities is the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information that is collected and recorded by HealthPaws App and how we use it.

Camera Access and Photo Storage

Our app requires access to your device's camera to allow you to take photos of your pet and save them within the application. These photos are only stored on your device and will not be transferred or stored elsewhere. The photos will be deleted when the application is removed from your device.

Log Files

HealthPaws App follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log users when they use our app. The information collected by log files includes your location, pet health data, date and time stamp, and possibly the number of clicks. This data is not linked to any information that is personally identifiable. The purpose of the information is for analyzing trends, administering the app, tracking users' movement on the app, and gathering demographic information.

Our Advertising Partners

Some of our advertisers on our app may use cookies and web beacons. Our advertising partners have their own Privacy Policy regarding their user data. For easier access, we will provide links to their Privacy Policies.
